Eco Serpents

Eco Serpents is an original game based on SDG 12, or Responsible Consumption and Production, created in Scratch to be showcased in University of San Carlos' Science and Technology Fair in 2024. In the game, you play as a snake that must consume garbage to gain points. All assets were drawn and coded by hand.

Budget and Expense Tracker

This program, created in C, allows users to manage their budgets and expenses through setting budget goals, recording their budgets and expenses throughout five categories including food, rent, entertainment, projects and utilities, as well as build trends and tables to showcase the users' financial habits. The program utilizes modularized functions, switch cases and TXT files in order to monitor and save users' inputs. This program was created as the final project for Computer Fundamentals in Programming 01 and received one of the highest scores out of the entire strand.
